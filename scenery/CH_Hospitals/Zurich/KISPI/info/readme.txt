Zürich Kinderspital Heliport HD 1.2
by Henry Favretto (aka Daikan)

Based on the original work by Pascal Küffer for FS2004, modified for X-Plane 10 with kind permission.


NOTES:
======
This scenery has been tested using:

- ZL16/17 orthophoto for +47+008 with HD mesh: http://simheaven.com/?page_id=8
- World2XPlane 0.5 beta: simheaven.com/experimental/


INSTALLATION:
=============
1. Unzip the directory "Custom Scenery" into your X-Plane home.
2. Adjust scenery load order as needed. See:
   http://wiki.x-plane.com/Prioritization 
   http://developer.x-plane.com/2012/09/scenery-packs-the-new-rules/
