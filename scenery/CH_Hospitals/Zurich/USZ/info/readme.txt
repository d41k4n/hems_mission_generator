Zürich Universitätsspital 1.3
Author: Henry Favretto (aka Daikan)

Based on the original work by Pascal Küffer for FS2004, modified for X-Plane 10/11 with kind permission.


PRE-REQUISITES:
===============

- Windows: Local admin privileges (for creating symlinks)
- Linux/MacOS: Extraction tool with .rar support (unrar, unar, Unarchiver etc.)
- X-Plane 10 or later
- Flyagi Vegetation Library 1.00 or later 
  https://forums.x-plane.org/index.php?/files/file/48047-flyagi-vegetation/
  

RECOMMENDED ADDON SCENERY (OPTIONAL):
=====================================

- Simheaven's X-Europe 3 or later
- Orthophoto mesh ZL16 or better


INSTALLATION:
=============

Windows:

- Download & run self-extracting .exe and follow instructions

Linux/MacOS:

- Download .rar archive and extract into X-Plane's "Custom Scenery" folder
