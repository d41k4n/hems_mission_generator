Basel Universitätsspital 1.1 
by Henry Favretto (aka Daikan)

Main buildings have been created based on publicly available data from Google 3D Warehouse.

The helipad objects were taken from the original work by Pascal Küffer for FS2004, modified for X-Plane 10 with kind permission.
