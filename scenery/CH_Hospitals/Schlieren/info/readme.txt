Schlieren Limmattal Spital HD 1.0 
by Henry Favretto (aka Daikan)

Based on the original work by Michael Schmidhauser for FS2004, modified for X-Plane 10 with kind permission.

REQUIREMENTS:
=============
- OpenSceneryX 2.0 or higher
- ZL17 Orthophoto HD for +47+008: http://simheaven.com/?page_id=208&wpfb_file=299 (1)

Optionally, but highly recommended:

- OSM Europe: http://simheaven.com/?page_id=208&wpfb_file=1129 (2)


INSTALLATION:
=============
1. Unzip the directory "Custom Scenery" into your X-Plane home.
2. Adjust scenery load order as needed. See:
   http://wiki.x-plane.com/Prioritization 
   http://developer.x-plane.com/2012/09/scenery-packs-the-new-rules/
